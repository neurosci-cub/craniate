What Are Sensory Illusions?

Sensory illusions occur when our brains interpret sensory information in ways that don't match objective reality. While optical illusions are most familiar, our other senses—taste, touch, smell, and hearing—are equally susceptible to being tricked. On this page, you'll explore fascinating experiments that reveal how your perception works and sometimes fails, offering insights into the incredible complexity of the human brain.
